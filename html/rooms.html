Our Rooms

1 Beds Room

Our 1 beds room is best for solo or couples who want a comfortable stay. This room has a comfortable bed, a clean bathroom, a smart TV, a minibar, and a free Wi-Fi. You can also view the skyscapers from large windows. Our 1 beds room is designed to make you feel relaxed through your day.

2 Beds Room

If you are with a friend or a family member, our 2 beds room is the perfect option to pick. This spacious room features two cozy beds, a private bathroom, a smart TV, a minibar, and free Wi-Fi. It has large windows with amazing view of skyscapers. Whether you need to work, relax, or sleep, our 2 beds room will make you feel like home.

Luxurious Suite

Our suite with 3 bedroom and a kitchen is the perfect option for you, if you are with many and prefer a luxurious stay. This room features three elegant bedrooms, each room with a king-size bed, a private bathroom, a smart TV, and a clean closet. You can also use the fully-equipped kitchen, the dining area, the living room, and the balcony with a wonderful view of skyscapers. This is the perfect choice for families or groups who want to feel the best in VhoTel.
